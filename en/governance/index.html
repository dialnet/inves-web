GOVERNANCE

A multi-level governance model to ensure coordinated and transparent decision-making

The INV.es project has a multi-level governance model designed to guarantee coordinated, transparent decision-making aligned with national science and research policies. This model allows combining institutional strategic vision with efficient technical and operational management, relying on the accumulated experience of the Dialnet Foundation and the active collaboration of participating universities.

A multi-level governance model

Coordination, transparency, and science policy

The governance of INV.es is structured in different complementary levels, each with clearly defined responsibilities, which allow addressing both strategic decisions of national scope as well as operational management and technological evolution of the portal.

This governance model ensures that INV.es establishes itself as a shared, sustainable, and constantly evolving infrastructure, at the service of the Spanish university system and the visibility of its scientific production.

Interuniversity project developed in collaboration with the Dialnet Foundation (University of La Rioja)
